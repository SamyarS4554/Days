🕰️ Mandela's Lifetime Timeline

1918: Born in Mvezo, South Africa
1944: Co-founded the African National Congress Youth League
1962: Arrested and sentenced to life imprisonment
1990: Released from prison after 27 years
1994: Became South Africa’s first Black president
2013: Passed away, leaving a legacy of peace and justice
